Our Story

Honestly Delicious.
Latin Flavors Made with Love.

Que Ricas — which means "how delicious" in Spanish — was born from chef Sahar Soleymani's desire to bring her Venezuelan heritage to Haddon Township. Growing up learning traditional family recipes, Sahar turned those flavors into a neighborhood gem.

We're a scratch kitchen through and through. Every empanada, every arepa, every salsa is made fresh daily with clean, quality ingredients — the kind of food that feels like home.
